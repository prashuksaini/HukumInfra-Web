Company Overview

HukumInfra is a leading manufacturer and supplier of high-quality scaffolding materials and formwork solutions. With years of expertise in the industry, we are committed to providing innovative and reliable products for construction projects across the globe. Our dedication to safety, durability, and efficiency has made us a trusted partner in the construction industry. Our product range includes cuplock scaffolding, H-frame scaffolding, tube & clamp scaffolding, ringlock scaffolding, and more. We also offer services such as manufacturing, rental services, on-site installation, and consultation to meet the diverse needs of our clients. Whether you are building a residential, commercial, or industrial project, HukumInfra has the right solutions for you.
