Adjustable Prop (MS Prop) Overview

HukumInfra‚Äôs Adjustable Prop, also known as **MS Prop**, is a **high-strength, telescopic support system** used for **temporary support in slab, beam, and wall formwork applications**. These props provide **adjustable height support**, making them essential for **reinforcing concrete structures** in various construction projects.

Constructed from **heavy-duty mild steel**, our **Adjustable Props** offer **exceptional load-bearing capacity** while allowing **precise height adjustments** for **slab shuttering, scaffolding, and framework stability**. The **telescopic inner and outer tube mechanism** allows quick adjustments, ensuring **flexibility for different structural requirements**.

With **multiple sizes, weight capacities, and locking mechanisms**, our **Adjustable Props** cater to **industrial, residential, and commercial construction**. Designed for **multiple reuses**, they help contractors **reduce costs and improve efficiency** while maintaining **safety standards**.
