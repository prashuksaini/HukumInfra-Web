Adjustable Prop (MS Prop) Overview

HukumInfra's Adjustable Props, also known as MS Props, are high-strength, telescopic support systems designed for providing temporary structural support in construction projects. These versatile props serve as the backbone of modern construction, offering reliable support for slab, beam, wall, and column formwork applications during the concrete curing process.

Constructed from premium-grade mild steel with precision engineering, our Adjustable Props deliver exceptional load-bearing capacity up to 150kN while enabling precise height adjustments through their innovative telescopic design. The two-part system consisting of inner and outer tubes with a threaded adjustment mechanism allows contractors to achieve exact height requirements with millimeter precision, ensuring perfect alignment and structural integrity in all construction scenarios.

HukumInfra's Adjustable Props come in various size ranges from 1.5m to 5.0m, catering to different construction needs from residential buildings to large-scale infrastructure projects. Each prop features a robust locking mechanism with safety pins for secure positioning and anti-corrosion treatment to withstand harsh construction environments. Our props are designed for quick assembly and dismantling, significantly reducing labor costs and project timelines while maintaining the highest safety standards.

Industry-Leading Features

HukumInfra's Adjustable Props are the preferred choice for construction professionals nationwide due to their superior engineering, industrial-grade materials, and comprehensive safety features. Each prop undergoes rigorous quality testing to ensure consistent performance under extreme load conditions, making them the most reliable support solution for critical concrete structures.

Superior Load Capacity

Engineered to support heavy concrete structures up to 150kN per unit, our props feature reinforced steel construction with optimized wall thickness to provide maximum stability during concrete setting. The wider base plate design ensures even load distribution, preventing sinking on softer ground surfaces.

Precision Height Adjustment

The telescopic inner tube with fine-thread adjustment mechanism allows for height variations from 1.5m to 5.0m, with millimeter-precise control. The quick-release pin system provides coarse adjustment, while the threaded sleeve mechanism offers fine-tuning capabilities for achieving perfect leveling.

Exceptional Durability

Manufactured using high-tensile MS tubing with reinforced walls, our props are designed for multiple use cycles exceeding 200 deployments. All components feature anti-rust treatments including zinc plating, galvanization, or powder coating, ensuring longevity even in moisture-heavy environments.

Versatile Application Range

Suitable for diverse construction scenarios including slab support, beam formwork, wall bracing, and column shuttering. The universal joint base plates and multi-angle head plates allow for adaptation to irregular surfaces and special construction requirements, making them ideal for both standard and complex architectural designs.

Advanced Locking Mechanism

Features a heavy-duty prop nut with positive locking system that prevents accidental height adjustments under load. The double-thread safety collar provides redundant security, while the quick-action handle allows for effortless adjustments even when wearing work gloves.

Modular Compatibility

Designed with universal connection points compatible with all major formwork systems. The standardized end fittings allow integration with H-frames, cup-lock systems, and traditional timber formwork, creating a versatile construction ecosystem that adapts to changing project requirements.

Construction Applications

HukumInfra's Adjustable Props provide essential support across various construction scenarios:

Slab Formwork Support

The primary application for Adjustable Props is supporting horizontal slab formwork during concrete pouring and curing. Props are typically arranged in grid patterns with appropriate spacing based on slab thickness and load calculations. They transfer the weight of wet concrete, reinforcement, and construction loads to the floor below while maintaining precise elevation control.

Beam Formwork Support

For concrete beam construction, Adjustable Props provide vertical support to beam bottom formwork and side shutters. The props can be configured with special U-head attachments to support beam soffit panels and ensure correct positioning during concrete pouring. Their adjustable nature allows for precise camber control when required by structural designs.

Wall Formwork Bracing

When used as inclined supports (typically at 45-60¬∞ angles), Adjustable Props provide essential bracing for wall formwork panels. This application prevents formwork movement or deflection during concrete placement, ensuring wall verticality and structural integrity. Special swivel heads allow for secure connections at various angles.

Column Formwork Support

For column construction, Adjustable Props provide lateral support to column formwork, preventing movement during concrete placement. Multiple props are typically arranged around column formwork in triangulated configurations to provide stability from all directions while allowing access for concrete pouring and vibration.

Temporary Structural Support

During renovation or retrofit projects, Adjustable Props provide temporary structural support for existing elements while modifications are made to supporting structures. This critical application requires precise load calculations and proper prop setup to ensure structural stability throughout the construction phase.

Prefabricated Element Installation

Adjustable Props serve as temporary supports during the installation and alignment of prefabricated concrete elements such as precast beams, wall panels, and floor slabs. Their precise height adjustment capability ensures perfect positioning before permanent connections are established.
