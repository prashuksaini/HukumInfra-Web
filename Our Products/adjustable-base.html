Adjustable Base (Base Jack) Overview

HukumInfra‚Äôs Adjustable Base, also known as the **Base Jack**, is a critical scaffolding component used to provide **superior stability and precise height adjustment** in construction projects. It serves as the foundation of scaffolding systems, ensuring **even load distribution** and a **stable working platform** on various surfaces.

Our **Base Jacks** are designed with a **threaded rod mechanism**, allowing workers to fine-tune the height of scaffolding structures, making them **essential for construction sites with uneven terrain**. Manufactured from **high-strength mild steel** and **galvanized for durability**, these jacks **prevent rust, corrosion, and deformation** under heavy loads.

The **adjustable height mechanism** makes the Base Jack an indispensable tool for **bridge construction, industrial sites, high-rise buildings, and metro projects**. With **multiple size variations**, our Base Jacks cater to the specific needs of each project while maintaining **compliance with international safety standards**.
