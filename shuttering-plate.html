Shuttering Plate Overview

HukumInfra offers premium-quality shuttering plates designed to provide exceptional concrete surface finishes while ensuring maximum durability and reusability. Our shuttering plates are a critical component of modern formwork systems, providing the direct contact surface for concrete placement.

Shuttering plates (also known as form-facing panels) are the crucial interface between concrete and formwork structure. They determine the texture, smoothness, and visual appearance of the finished concrete while withstanding the full pressure of wet concrete. Our range includes both film-faced plywood and steel shuttering plates engineered for various construction applications, from residential to heavy infrastructure projects.

Whether you're constructing walls, columns, slabs, or specialized concrete elements, our shuttering plates deliver consistent results with minimal maintenance requirements and extended service life.

Superior Finish Quality

Our shuttering plates are engineered to produce exceptional concrete surface finishes with minimal defects, reducing or eliminating the need for post-concrete finishing work.

Extended Reusability

Manufactured with premium materials and protective coatings, our shuttering plates can withstand numerous concrete pours without degradation, offering excellent return on investment.

High Pressure Resistance

Designed to withstand the significant hydrostatic pressure of wet concrete without deformation, ensuring dimensional accuracy of finished structures.

Easy Maintenance

Special surface treatments make our shuttering plates easy to clean and maintain between uses, with excellent release properties that prevent concrete adhesion.
